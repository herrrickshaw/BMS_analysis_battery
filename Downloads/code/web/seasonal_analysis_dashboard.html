📌 Key Seasonal Insights
Peak Season (Oct-Mar): Higher traffic due to festival season (Diwali, Christmas) and post-monsoon harvest movement. February-March shows highest collections.
Low Season (Jun-Jul): Monsoon impact reduces traffic significantly by 13-15%. Travel restrictions and road maintenance activities contribute.
Value vs Volume Growth: NIIF data shows value growth (11.9%) exceeds volume growth (6.8%), indicating toll rate increases > traffic growth.
State Concentration: Top 5 states (UP, RJ, TN, MH, KA) account for ~50% of toll traffic. UP and RJ lead in revenue generation.
Freight Importance: Commercial vehicles account for large value share despite lower volume than passenger vehicles.
