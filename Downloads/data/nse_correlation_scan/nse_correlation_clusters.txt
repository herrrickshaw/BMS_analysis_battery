The raw scan above includes ~2,225 NSE-listed instruments, many of which
are ETFs/index funds rather than operating companies (Nifty/Sensex/sector
ETFs, gold/silver ETFs, liquid/money-market funds). These trivially
correlate ~0.99 with other funds tracking the same underlying index or
commodity, which:
  (a) dominates the raw "top correlated pairs" list with fund-vs-fund pairs
      that aren't a meaningful "these stocks move together" finding, and
  (b) contributes to one giant 1,264-instrument connected-component at
      threshold=0.55 -- an artifact of transitive chaining through broad
      market beta across a very large, densely-connected universe, not a
      genuine single mega-cluster.
