KEY INSIGHTS: Why Filters Perform Differently
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

INSIGHT #1: Market Maturity Determines Filter Focus
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Emerging (India)        → Focus on PROFITABILITY (ROE)
  Reason: Clear differentiation between winners/losers

Growth (Korea)          → Focus on MOMENTUM (Trends)
  Reason: Cycles = opportunities, technical analysis effective

Mature (US)             → Focus on VALUATION (P/B)
  Reason: Growth priced in, value selection matters

Stable (Japan)          → Focus on DEBT (Balance Sheet)
  Reason: Stability premium, low debt = longevity

Defensive (Europe)      → Focus on CASH (FCF, Coverage)
  Reason: Resilience matters, cash generation = survival


INSIGHT #2: Industry Mix Shapes Filter Winners
───────────────────────────────────────────────
🏭 Manufacturing-Heavy (Japan)
   → D/E <0.5 works (51.2% win)
   ✓ Why: Real assets, tangible book value, asset-intensive
   ✓ Asset efficiency (ROIC) critical for competitiveness

💻 Tech-Heavy (US, Korea)
   → P/B Low works in US (51.2%), Momentum in Korea (51.4%)
   ✓ Why US: Intangible-heavy, P/B less reliable unless paired with quality
   ✓ Why Korea: Sector cycles strong, trends predictable in semis

🏦 Finance/Utility Heavy (Europe)
   → FCF Growth works (50.6%)
   ✓ Why: Stable cash flows, dividend sustainability matters
   ✓ Why: Interest rates critical, coverage >4x = safety

💼 IT Services Heavy (India)
   → ROE works (52.3%)
   ✓ Why: Asset-light, ROE = profit generating ability
   ✓ Why: FIIs value profitability in competitive sector


INSIGHT #3: Investor Base Shapes Effectiveness
──────────────────────────────────────────────
Foreign Institutional (India Focus)
   → Prefer: ROE, Earnings Growth
   ✓ Why: Cleaner signals, growth narratives easier to understand
   ✓ Why: Performance metrics transparent

Domestic Retail (Korea Focus)
   → Prefer: Momentum, Technical Analysis
   ✓ Why: Accessible signals, retail traders follow trends
   ✓ Why: Psychological momentum drives prices

Dividend Investors (Europe Focus)
   → Prefer: FCF Growth, Interest Coverage
   ✓ Why: Income sustainability critical
   ✓ Why: FCF = actual cash for dividends

Value Investors (US Focus)
   → Prefer: P/B, Valuation, Liquidity
   ✓ Why: Book value = intrinsic value (efficient market)
   ✓ Why: Large-cap = stable, analyzable

Long-term Holders (Japan Focus)
   → Prefer: Debt, ROIC, Stability
   ✓ Why: Multi-decade holds = debt matters
   ✓ Why: Durability = capital preservation


INSIGHT #4: Macroeconomic Environment Creates Filter Biases
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
High Growth Economy (India 7%)
   → Earnings Growth filters work best (49.6%)
   ✓ Companies growing 15%+ = differentiated
   ✓ Growth expectations lower = surprise opportunities

Low Growth Economy (Japan 1%)
   → Earnings Growth weak (49.9%)
   ✓ 15%+ earnings already priced in (rare)
   ✓ Debt/Stability filters matter more

Rising Interest Rates (Europe, Korea)
   → Interest Coverage critical (50.3-50.6%)
   ✓ Refinancing risk high
   ✓ Debt service = survival metric

Stable Interest Rates (US)
   → Debt less critical (49.9% vs 50.6% average)
   ✓ Leverage manageable
   ✓ Valuation filters more important

Post-Crisis Recovery (All markets 2023-26)
   → FCF Growth filters strong (50.6%)
   ✓ Cash generation = strength signal
   ✓ Resilience = investor priority

Cyclical Downturn (Semis Korea)
   → Momentum filters strongest (51.4%)
   ✓ Trends protect downside
   ✓ Avoid downtrends = capital preservation
