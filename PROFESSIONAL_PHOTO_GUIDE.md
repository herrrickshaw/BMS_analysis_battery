# Professional Photo Guide for Your Profiles

## 🎯 Why Professional Photos Matter

A professional headshot increases LinkedIn profile views by **21%** and improves credibility across all platforms. Given your profile as a Business Development Executive and Quantitative Investor, a strong professional photo is essential.

---

## 📸 Professional Headshot Requirements

### Ideal Specifications

**Technical:**
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200x1500 pixels (or 1:1.25 aspect ratio)
- **File Format:** JPG or PNG (upload-friendly)
- **File Size:** 1-5 MB (web-optimized)
- **Background:** Solid, light neutral (white, light gray, light blue)
- **Lighting:** Professional studio lighting (no harsh shadows)

**Framing:**
- **Crop:** Shoulders up (head & shoulders portrait)
- **Distance:** Close enough to see facial details clearly
- **Angle:** Slightly above eye level (professional angle)
- **Position:** Centered in frame

**Appearance:**
- **Dress Code:** Business formal (blazer/shirt for professional look)
- **Colors:** Dark blazer with light shirt (blue, white, gray recommended)
- **Grooming:** Clean, professional hairstyle
- **Expression:** Natural smile (confident, approachable)
- **Eye Contact:** Direct to camera

---

## ✅ Platform-Specific Requirements

### LinkedIn Profile Photo
- **Size:** 400x500 pixels (recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5
- **Background:** Professional (neutral or soft)
- **Dress:** Business formal
- **Impact:** Most important profile photo

### GitHub Profile Photo
- **Size:** 120x120 pixels minimum
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **Background:** Can be more casual/creative
- **Dress:** Business casual acceptable
- **Impact:** Professional but can show personality

### Personal Website/Portfolio
- **Size:** Flexible (recommend 300x400 or 400x500)
- **Background:** Professional or branded
- **Dress:** Business formal
- **Impact:** Critical for credibility

### Email Signature
- **Size:** Small (100x100 to 150x150 pixels)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square) or 16:9
- **Background:** Professional
- **Impact:** Moderate

## 🎨 Photo Editing Guidelines

- ❌ Cartoon effects
- ❌ Casual/lifestyle photos

## ✨ Quick DIY Option (This Week)

If you need a photo immediately:

1. **Find good natural lighting:**
   - Window with diffused light (not direct sun)
   - Overcast day is ideal
   - Avoid harsh shadows

2. **Background:**
   - Plain white wall, light curtain, or light gray backdrop
   - Keep 2-3 feet away from background (soft focus)

3. **Clothing:**
   - Dark blazer + light dress shirt
   - Well-fitted, no wrinkles

4. **Setup:**
   - Phone on tripod at chest height, tilted slightly up
   - Or ask colleague/friend to take photo

5. **Poses:**
   - Take 50+ photos with variations:
     - Different angles
     - Different expressions
     - Different clothing options

6. **Editing:**
   - Use **RemoveBG.com** (free) to remove background
   - Use **Photoshop** or **Canva** (free version) to add neutral background
   - Use **Fotor** or **Pixlr** for quick enhancements

7. **Result:**
   - Professional-looking photo in 2-3 hours
