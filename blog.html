Features

Built for serious research

Every component engineered to surface high-conviction ideas across all major markets, at speed.

📊
Darvas / Buffett Scanner
10-point scoring: near 52W high, above EMA-50/200, MACD confirmation, volume surge, RSI health, and Buffett ROE/PE/D·E overlays. BUY ≥ 5 · WATCH ≥ 3.
🔬
Piotroski F-Score
8-point financial strength: profitability, leverage, and operating efficiency combined with momentum proxies. Dynamic thresholds scale with data availability.
⚡
Cassandra Quote Engine
Apache Cassandra caches OHLCV, RSI, EMA-50/200, MACD, volume metrics, fundamentals and 6 period returns for 17,000+ instruments across all markets.
📰
Live News Integration
Per-stock news via yfinance and sector news from Google News RSS — no API key. Lazy-loaded on row expand, 30-min cache to respect rate limits.
💼
Portfolio P&L
Upload Excel or PDF holdings. Tickers auto-extracted, historical prices fetched, dividends included. P&L per position + total return + RSI signals.
🌍
Sector Benchmarks
Damodaran / NYU Stern sector PE, PB, EV/EBITDA, ROE and margin data for India, US and Europe — updated January 2025.
📈
Period Returns
1D, 1W, 1M, 3M, 6M and 1Y returns computed free from the existing 1-year OHLCV close series — zero extra API calls.
⚗️
Put-Call Parity Arb
Live trading engine for BankNifty, Crude Oil & Silver derivatives. Signals only when (C−P) − (F−K)e^(−rT) exceeds all-in transaction costs.
