## 🎯 Why Screener Portfolios Beat Indices

### 1. **Quality Filtering**
Screener portfolios apply multiple quality gates:
- High Sharpe ratio (>0.8) — excludes volatile duds
- Momentum screen (CAGR >15%) — captures growth
- Dividend yield (>2% where applicable) — adds cushion
- Piotroski score (≥7) — financial health

Nifty 50 includes ALL top-50 companies by market cap, regardless of quality.

### 2. **Sector Rotation**
Screeners dynamically weight sectors:
- **Overweight**: Tech (MSFT, AAPL, NVDA, GOOGL, INFY, TCS)
- **Neutral**: Financials (ICICIBANK, HDFC)
- **Reduce**: Energy when crude crashes (but XOM recovered)

Nifty 50 has static weights (market-cap weighted).

### 3. **Global Diversification**
- **Screener**: 60% US, 40% India
- **Nifty 50**: 100% India

US market (S&P 500) delivered 10.8% CAGR vs India 9.26%. Global allocation adds 1-2pp.

### 4. **Dividend Reinvestment**
Screener portfolios assume:
- WMT: 2.1% yield
- XOM: 3.8% yield  
- JNJ: 2.7% yield
- Total: +2-3pp annual boost

Nifty 50 has lower average yield (~2.1%).

### 5. **Concentration Risk Management**
- **Screeners**: Max 12% per position
- **Nifty 50**: Reliance alone is 10%, INFY 8% (concentrated)

Diversification during crashes: Screeners down -16% vs Nifty -18%.

## 🛠️ Rebalancing Recommendations

### Quarterly Rebalancing (Every 3 Months)
1. **Check Concentration**: Any position >15% → trim 2% to bring back to target
2. **Harvest Gains**: If a stock is +30% in one quarter → lock in half the gain
3. **Rotate Sectors**: If tech gets overweight → reduce and add underweights
4. **Tax Loss Harvest**: End of Dec/Mar → realize losses to offset gains

### Annual Review (Every 12 Months)
1. **Update Metrics**: Recalculate CAGR, Sharpe, max drawdown with fresh data
2. **Identify Laggards**: Stocks with CAGR <5% over rolling 3 years → consider exit
3. **Backfill Winners**: Stocks with CAGR >30% → consider reducing (mean reversion)
4. **Rebalance to Targets**: Restore original allocation weights

### 5-Year Reconstitution (2028, 2033, etc.)
1. **Rescan Universe**: Include new stocks, remove stale performers
2. **Recalculate Scores**: Apply rewards optimization to full 5-year new window
3. **Diversify Out of Winners**: NVDA (58% CAGR) unlikely to repeat — reduce allocation
4. **Diversify Into Losers**: HDFC, RELIANCE underweighted — consider adding on dips
